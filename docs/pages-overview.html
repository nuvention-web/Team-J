Team Mission:

To connect communities and build empowerment within these communities through the power of social networks.

Team Current Focus:

Our current focus is to build a social network platform for star professors and students to streamline the connection process.

Product Space and Customer Focus:

For Star Professors and all students in Northwestern University.

Students ask questions (usually outside their majors) and want to hear from the best Prof. They can address problems to specific Prof or to the open space. Prof can see questions that are addressed to them, or questions that are in the open space. They can pick the questions they want to answer. Answers should be shorter than 2 min and in audio/video format. Every user (Profs and Students) can see all the questions.
