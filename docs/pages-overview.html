Team Mission:

To leverage the power of social networks to promote knowledge sharing and collaboration within large, extended communities such as universities.

Current Focus:

As a team, our current focus is to build a social network within university communities. This social network will act as a collaborative, engaging platform that directly connects professors (and related scholars) with students in need.

Product Space:

Education ( Currently limited to the Northwestern University community)

Customer Focus:

Imagine you are a student studying at Northwestern University, and would like to get in touch with a professor whom you may know or have no connection with whatsoever to learn more about a topic or for help on an assignment. However, maybe you do not have direct access, or you’ve got to work two jobs, or your class size is so large that you are unable to meet with your professor one on one during office hours. Maybe you have anxiety, and are hesitant to approach them directly and are unaware of their schedules. Does that mean you shouldn’t reach out to them?

Not at all!

With Answerly, we bring the two worlds together. Answerly is a multimedia educational platform where students can address problems to specific professors and scholars within the Northwestern University or to the open space at large. Professors and scholars can view the questions that are posed, and respond accordingly. Answers should be shorter than 2 minutes long and in audio/video format.

With Answerly, a collaborative learning environment without barriers is finally within our reach. Students are no longer limited by their socio-economic, physical or mental hurdles in their pursuit of knowledge. Answerly takes advantage of the vast, stalwart academic faculty within the university, and actively engage them to benefit the learning of our student community.
