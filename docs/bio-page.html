About Us

Reedhima Mandlik is a Master’s student studying Media Innovation and Entrepreneurship at Northwestern University. Her undergraduate background is a double major in psychology and journalism, also from Northwestern University. Reedhima has studied international political science at the University of Cambridge in England, and has work experience at Facebook and, most recently, Salesforce. She specializes in content creation/analytics, audience insight, UX/UI research, and innovation design. When not at school or work, Reedhima is always on the hunt for the best coffee and donuts in Chicagoland.

Akshita Gupta is studying her Master’s in IMC at Northwestern University. She has travelled, and have lived in several geographies across the globe - India, UK and now Chicago. Her professional experience includes having worked with a digital marketing & strategy solutions company start-up, after which she assisted in her family run business, playing a critical role in business transition from a traditional family-run business to a professional organization.
